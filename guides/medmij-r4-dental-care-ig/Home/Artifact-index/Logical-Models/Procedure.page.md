# Procedure

The Procedure CIM is largely based on the zib [Procedure](https://zibs.nl/wiki/Procedure-v5.2(2020EN)) from publication 2020, but uses other (dental care-specific) terminology to indicate the procedure type. Moreover, the MedicalDevice (NL-CM:14.1.7) and Requester (NL-CM:14.1.10) concepts from the zib have been omitted.
